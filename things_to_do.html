Food and drink

Tanzanian cuisine varies by geographical region. Along the coastal regions (Dar es Salaam, Tanga, Bagamoyo, Zanzibar, and Pemba), spicy foods are common, and there is also much use of coconut milk. Explore Restaturants

Tanzania makes for a wondrous vacation in the lap of nature. The locals are friendly and you would find a pleasurable mix of cultures, food and people in the country. The main cities of Dodoma, Dar es Salaam, Zanzibar, and Moshi are heavily visited by tourists, which is why finding restaurants in these areas is easier. You would find a range of dishes from steaks and burgers to the local seafood as well as Indian cuisine (surprisingly) in restaurants in Tanzania.

Tours

Tanzania is home to some of Africa's most famous national parks and natural attractions, including majestic Mount Kilimanjaro. Consequently, the most popular things to do in Tanzania and the reason many people visit the country, are the safaris and wildlife-related adventures. Most visitors will find themselves passing through Dar es Salaam and heading out to the wilderness areas and other destinations. For those who want to spend some time soaking up the sun, the beautiful beaches of Zanzibar beckon.
